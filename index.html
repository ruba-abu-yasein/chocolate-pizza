Chocolate Pizza

POSTED ON 15 DEC 2013 / DESSERTS

Preheat oven to 450°. Sprinkle about half the sugar onto a clean surface. Place pizza dough on sugar and spread remaining sugar on top. Roll pizza dough out into an 11" circle. Transfer dough to a large baking sheet and poke all over with a fork, then brush with melted butter. Bake until golden, 12 to 15 minutes. Let cool slightly. Spread Nutella all over crust, leaving a 1" border around the edge. Top with chopped Reese’s Peanut Butter Cups, Reese’s Pieces, and peanuts. Serve warm.

3 tbsp. granulated sugar, , 8 oz. pizza dough ,2 tbsp. melted butter ,1/2 c. Nutella , 1/2 c. chopped Reese's Peanut Butter Cups , 1/2 c. Reese's Pieces , 1/4 c. peanuts.Preheat oven to 450°. Sprinkle about half the sugar onto a clean surface. Place pizza dough on sugar and spread remaining sugar on top. Roll pizza dough out into an 11" circle. Transfer dough to a large baking sheet and poke all over with a fork, then brush with melted butter. Bake until golden, 12 to 15 minutes. Let cool slightly. Spread Nutella all over crust, leaving a 1" border around the edge. Top with chopped Reese’s Peanut Butter Cups, Reese’s Pieces, and peanuts. Serve warm.
